AstroGuru Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 21, 2026

App name: AstroGuru

Package: com.astroguru.horoscope

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how AstroGuru collects, uses, stores, and protects user information. AstroGuru provides daily horoscope, kundli, panchang, numerology, compatibility, and related astrology features.

2. Information We May Collect

Depending on how you use the app, AstroGuru may collect and store the following information:

  • Name
  • Date of birth
  • Birth time
  • Birth place
  • Language preference
  • Notification preferences
  • Locally generated astrology results and settings

3. How We Use Information

Your information is used to:

  • Generate kundli, panchang, and astrology insights
  • Personalize your horoscope experience
  • Store your profile and preferences on your device
  • Schedule daily horoscope notifications if you enable them
  • Create share cards, PDF reports, and exported content when requested by you

4. Local Storage and Processing

AstroGuru primarily stores user profile information and astrology inputs locally on your device. The main astrology engine in the app operates offline for core calculations. Under normal app usage, your birth data is not uploaded to our own backend servers.

5. Advertising and Third-Party Services

AstroGuru may use third-party services such as Google AdMob to display advertisements. These services may collect device identifiers, usage data, advertising identifiers, and related diagnostic information according to their own privacy policies.

If ads are enabled in the app, Google privacy terms may apply in addition to this policy.

6. Notifications

If you grant notification permission, AstroGuru may send daily horoscope reminders or similar alerts. You can disable notifications at any time from the app settings or your device settings.

7. Sharing, Files, and Device Features

The app may use device features such as local storage, PDF generation, screenshots, share sheets, and other export actions so that you can save or share content generated within the app. Once you share content outside the app, the receiving app or service may process that data under its own rules.

8. Children’s Privacy

AstroGuru is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children as a separate category. If you believe inappropriate information was entered into the app, the locally stored data can be removed by clearing the app data from the device.

9. Your Control and Choices

You can:

  • Edit profile information inside the app
  • Update birth details and preferences
  • Disable notifications
  • Remove locally stored data by clearing app data from your device

10. Data Security

We aim to use reasonable measures to protect information stored within the app environment. However, no method of electronic storage or device usage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect app changes, advertising configuration, legal requirements, or feature improvements. Updated versions may be published through GitHub Pages, the app repository, or the Google Play listing.

12. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please use the developer contact information available on the Google Play Store listing for AstroGuru.
